# Features & Pages

This portfolio includes a wide range of interactive, responsive pages tailored for a professional developer persona.

---

## 🏠 Home Page
The primary landing experience. It features:
- **Hero Section**: High-impact introduction with professional branding.
- **Collaboration Section**: Focused on being "Open to Work", with links to LinkedIn and professional 3D imagery.
- **Resume Feature**: A direct link to download the latest professional CV (`resume jan 12.pdf`).

---

## 👨‍💻 About Page
A detailed view of the professional and academic journey.
- **Timeline**: A "tech-forward" visual representation of career and education milestones.
- **Custom Illustrations**: Contextual images showing workplace environments and personal interests.
- **Interactive Nodes**: Hover effects that highlight specific achievements on the timeline.

---

## 🧪 Research Page
A dedicated space highlighting technical research and academic excellence.
- **Research Hero**: Action-oriented content showing the intersection of AI, ML, and software engineering.
- **Tech Stack Collage**: A visual overview of tools like AWS, React, etc., to demonstrate research capabilities.

---

## 🛠️ Projects Page
A gallery of professional and personal projects.
- **Categorization**: Projects can be categorized for easier navigation.
- **Action Links**: Direct paths to "Live Demo" and "Source Code" for each project.
- **Modern Cards**: A card-based layout using Radix primitives to ensure clarity and accessibility.

---

## 📊 Skills Page
A comprehensive look at technical proficiencies.
- **Data-Driven Views**: Uses visual charts to show proficiency in different domains.
- **Technical Visuals**: Custom-generated tech stacks to demonstrate branding and technical range.

---

## 👤 Personal Page
A space focused on personal brand, interests, and hobbies to humanize the developer persona.

---

## 📧 Contact Page
A direct communication hub for recruiters and collaborators.
- **Social Links**: Centralized links for GitHub, LinkedIn, and Email.
- **User-Friendly Spacing**: Optimized layout for quick readability.
- **GitHub Integration**: Links to the user's personal profile.
